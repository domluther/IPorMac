Address Format Rules:

  • IPv4: 4 decimal numbers (0-255) separated by dots
    Example: 192.168.1.1
  • IPv6: 8 groups of 4 hex digits separated by colons. Groups can be empty or compressed.
    Example: 2001:0db8:85a3:0000:0000:8a2e:0370:7334
    Compressed: 2001:db8::8a2e:370:7334
  • MAC: 6 pairs of hex digits separated by colons or dashes
    Example: 00:1A:2B:3C:4D:5E or 00-1A-2B-3C-4D-5E
